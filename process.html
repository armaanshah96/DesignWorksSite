Our Process.

Let's make magic happen.

  • 1.

    Initial Contact: After you fill out a DesignWorks specifications form, we’ll respond within 24 hours with a price quote, contract, and timeline information.

  • 2.

    Draft Stage: We’ll pair you with a carefully chosen designer/developer and complete initial designs or high-fidelity mock-ups by an agreed-upon due date.

  • 3.

    The Revision Round: You can interact with your designer/developer online to implement detailed revisions.

  • 4.

    Your Final Product: Once you make your payment, we’ll deliver your final website or non-watermarked design.
